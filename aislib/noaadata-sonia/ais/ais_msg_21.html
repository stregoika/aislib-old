AIS Message: AidsToNavReport (::)

Description:

Used by a station mounted on an aid-to-navigation. Note IALA A-124 P.19 for more bits than are here. Also covered in A-126 in ANNEX 1 - MESSAGE 21 in IALA Recommendation A-126 on the use of AIS in Marine Aids to Navigation Serves Ed 1.1. This has optional extensions.
